New to a State? Complete DMV Transfer Guide for New Residents

Everything you need to do at the DMV after moving to a new state — deadlines, fees, and what transfers automatically.

⚠️ Warning

California has the strictest deadline — only 10 days to transfer your license.

Steps to Transfer Your Driver's License

  1. Gather documents: out-of-state license, proof of new address, SSN proof
  2. Schedule DMV appointment
  3. Take a vision test (knowledge test may be waived)
  4. Pay transfer fee and surrender your old license

Does My Car Registration Transfer Automatically?

No — you must register your vehicle in the new state within the deadline. You'll typically need to get a VIN inspection and may need an emissions test.
