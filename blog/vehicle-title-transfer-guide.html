Vehicle Title Transfer Guide

Step-by-step title transfer for buying, selling, gifting, or inheriting a vehicle.

When is a Title Transfer Needed?

  • Buying or selling a used vehicle
  • Receiving a vehicle as a gift
  • Inheriting a vehicle from a deceased person
  • Adding or removing a co-owner
  • Paying off a vehicle loan (lien release)

Required Documents

  • Signed title from seller (all owners must sign)
  • Odometer disclosure statement (vehicles under 10 years old)
  • Bill of sale (recommended — protects both buyer and seller)
  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Lien release letter if there was a loan on the vehicle
  • Payment for title fees and sales tax

Fees

State title transfer fee: $15–$75. Plus state sales tax on the purchase price (typically 5–10%). California title fee: $21. Texas: $33. New York: $50.

Timeline Warning

Must be completed within 10–30 days after purchase (varies by state). Late transfers result in penalties.
