How to Transfer a Car Title — Complete Guide for Buyers & Sellers

Whether you're buying, selling, gifting, or inheriting a vehicle, here is the complete checklist to transfer a car title correctly.

Seller Checklist

  • Sign the back of the title
  • Fill in the odometer reading
  • Write the sale price
  • Remove your license plates
  • File a Release of Liability with the DMV

Buyer Checklist

  • Verify VIN matches the vehicle
  • Check for liens on the title
  • Submit title transfer within 10–30 days (varies by state)
  • Bring ID, signed title, bill of sale, and payment

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not checking for liens before purchasing
  • Missing the transfer deadline and paying late fees
  • Not filing a Release of Liability (seller can be liable for tickets)
  • Incorrect odometer reading on the title

Gifting a Vehicle

Most states allow title transfers as gifts with reduced or waived sales tax. You'll typically need a gift affidavit and the standard title transfer documents. Check your state's specific requirements.
