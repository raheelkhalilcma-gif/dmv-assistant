REAL ID — Complete Guide for All 50 States (2026)

📅 April 1, 2026⏱ 8 min read🏷 REAL ID

As of May 7, 2025, a REAL ID is required for domestic air travel and entering federal facilities.

What Is a REAL ID?

A REAL ID is a state-issued driver's license or ID card that meets federal security standards. It has a star marking in the upper corner.

💡 Pro Tip: If you have a US passport or military ID, you can use those instead for domestic flights.

Documents You'll Need

  • ✅ Proof of identity (birth certificate, US passport)
  • ✅ Proof of SSN (SSN card, W-2)
  • ✅ Two proofs of state residency
  • ✅ Name change documents if applicable
